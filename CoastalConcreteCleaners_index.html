About Us

Coastal Concrete Cleaners is proudly owned and operated by Ty Garrett in Theodore, Alabama. We specialize in residential homes, small commercial storefronts, Airbnb properties, and vacation rentals. Whether it’s your driveway, sidewalk, steps, or patio, I treat every job like it’s my own home.

Our services are limited to walkable concrete surfaces — we do not clean loading zones, parking lots, or large commercial spaces.

What sets us apart is our commitment to honest communication, attention to detail, and the pride we take in keeping our local communities clean — one surface at a time.

Thank you for supporting a local business rooted in quality and professionalism.
