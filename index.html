Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical cleaning take?

Most residential driveway or walkway cleanings take betWeen 1 to 3 hours depending on size and condition.

Do you use any chemicals during cleaning?

We primarily use high-pressure water to clean concrete. For heavy mold or algae, We may use environmentally safe cleaning agents.

Can you remove oil stains?

We can help reduce oil stains, but deep or old stains may require specialized treatment beyond pressure washing.
